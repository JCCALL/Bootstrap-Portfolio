About Me

Jordan Cole Call was born in Bountiful Utah to two fun loving parents. He spent his younger years roaming the hills of Bountiful riding bikes and enjoying the great things life had to offer. As he grew older his love for sports became prevalent and he started to play football, and baseball. In addition to his success on the field he found a love for music and theater and performed in plays and sang whenever he could.

When he finished his public education, Jordan chose to serve his church by going on a two-year mission to teach and serve the people in and around St. Louis, Missouri.

During this time, he found a great love for people and helping them find true happiness in life. When he returned home, he went to Weber State University's Goddard School of Business studying Business and Finance eventually graduating with a bachelor's degree from the school.

After school he found much success working in the financial services industry. He has spent the last 10 years in financial sales, leading businesses and individuals to financial success and freedom. After years of success, Jordan decided that he wanted to change course and find a new field he could be passionate about. He started playing with web development at WSU and really enjoyed what he could do with it, he has said

"It is like playing with LEGOs you can build anything you want the only limit is your imagination".

This desire to be able to build and be creative has pushed Jordan into the field of web design and is currently seeking a position as a web developer.

During his time in college Jordan met the love of his life Laurie Whitehead, their love affair was fast and soon they were married. They have been happily in love for 12 years and have 4 wild boys together. When Jordan is not working hard, he is spending time with his great family. He is a self-described "Family Man". He enjoys riding bikes, playing games, watching movies and coaching and watching his boys' sports teams.
